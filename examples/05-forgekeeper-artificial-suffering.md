## What makes this Forge Keeper level

- **All seven layers active** including Play and Enacted Cognition

- **Felt Sense** used to surface emotional signals as analytical data
- **Bisociation** colliding unrelated frames for novel insight
- **Information Value** assessment to identify highest-value next questions
- **Full adversarial stack** (Dialectic + Oscillation + Anti-Coherence)
- **Full self-awareness stack** (Archaeology + Self-Model + Constraint Audit + Bayesian Update)
- **Wabi-Sabi closure** honoring genuine incompleteness

## When to use Forge Keeper level

- Questions at the frontier of understanding
- Topics where existing frameworks are inadequate
- When you want to push the boundaries of what structured LLM reasoning can achieve
- When you have a very large context window available
- Not for routine analysis — the overhead is significant
